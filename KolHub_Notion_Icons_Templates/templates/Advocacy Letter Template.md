# Advocacy Letter – Template

**Recipient (insurance/social worker):**  
**Subject:** Request for written explanation / accommodation

Dear [Name],

I’m requesting a written explanation of [decision/denial] so I can review and respond appropriately. My condition (hEDS/POTS/CPTSD) substantially limits my daily living and requires [accommodation].

Please provide the full rationale, applicable policies, and next steps in writing.

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]

_Tone options:_ Calm | Firm | Warm
