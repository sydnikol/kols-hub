# Content Monetization Quick Start Guide

Transform your writing and photography into passive income in 5 minutes!

## Step 1: Connect Google Services (1 min)

1. Navigate to `/content-monetization` in the app
2. Click **"Connect Google Account"** button
3. Authorize access to:
   - Google Drive (for writing)
   - Google Photos (for photography)
4. Wait for the green "Connected" status

## Step 2: Scan Your Content (2 min)

### For Writers:

1. Click **"Scan Writing"** button
2. System searches for:
   - `.docx` files (Word documents)
   - `.txt` files (Plain text)
   - `.md` files (Markdown)
   - `.pdf` files (PDFs)
3. Wait for scan to complete
4. View discovered content

**What gets scanned:**
- Blog posts
- Articles
- Essays
- Stories
- Books/chapters
- Any text document

### For Photographers:

1. Click **"Scan Photos"** button
2. System filters for:
   - High-resolution images (1920x1080+)
   - Categories: nature, portraits, urban, food, etc.
3. Wait for scan to complete
4. View discovered photos

**Quality requirements:**
- Minimum: 1920x1080 (HD)
- Recommended: 3000x2000+ (6MP+)
- Commercial quality preferred

## Step 4: Publish Your Content

### Writing Platforms:

**Medium** (Best for: Articles, essays)
- Click "Publish" on writing card
- Copy metadata and content
- Go to medium.com
- Create new story
- Paste content
- Submit to publication

**Substack** (Best for: Newsletters, series)
- Click "Publish" on writing card
- Use suggested title and content
- Go to your Substack
- Create new post
- Configure for subscribers

**Kindle** (Best for: Books, long-form)
- Click "Publish" on writing card
- Export to .docx format
- Use Kindle Create tool
- Upload to KDP dashboard

### Photography Platforms:

**Shutterstock** (General stock)
- Click "Upload" on photo card
- Copy generated metadata
- Go to submit.shutterstock.com
- Upload photo
- Paste title, description, keywords
- Submit for review

**Adobe Stock** (Premium quality)
- Click "Upload" on photo card
- Use metadata provided
- Go to contributor.stock.adobe.com
- Upload photo with metadata
- Submit

**Getty Images** (Premium/editorial)
- Application required first
- Once approved, use upload metadata
- Submit through ESP platform

## Step 5: Track Earnings

### Update Earnings Manually:

When you get paid by a platform:

1. Find the content piece
2. Note platform and amount
3. Service will track:
   - Total earnings per piece
   - Platform performance
   - ROI (time invested vs earnings)
   - Monthly revenue

### Portfolio Dashboard Shows:

- **Total Earnings** - All-time across all content
- **Monthly Revenue** - Current month earnings
- **Content Count** - Number of monetized pieces
- **Top Platforms** - Best performing platforms
- **Average Quality** - Portfolio quality score

## Monetization Strategies

### Quick Wins (First Week)

**For Writers:**
1. Start with Medium Partner Program
2. Pick your 3 best articles
3. Optimize titles with suggested keywords
4. Publish and share

**For Photographers:**
1. Select 20-30 high-quality photos
2. Upload to Shutterstock first (easiest approval)
3. Use generated metadata
4. Add to other platforms once approved

### Building Income (First Month)

**Writers:**
- Publish 1-2 articles per week
- Build Medium following
- Start Substack newsletter
- Compile book from blog posts

**Photographers:**
- Upload 50-100 photos
- Submit to 3+ platforms
- Focus on commercial categories
- Add model releases for people photos

### Scaling Up (3-6 Months)

**Writers:**
- 50+ published pieces
- Multiple platforms
- Newsletter with 100+ subscribers
- Published book on Kindle

**Photographers:**
- 200+ photo portfolio
- Approved on premium platforms (Getty, Adobe)
- Niche specialization
- Exclusive licensing deals
