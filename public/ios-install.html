  • All 10,000+ features

Why PWA instead of App Store?

Kol's Hub is a Progressive Web App (PWA) - a modern app technology that gives you all the benefits of a native app without needing to download from the App Store. It's faster to install, takes less storage, and always stays up-to-date!
