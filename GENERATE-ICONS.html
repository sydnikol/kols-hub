  1. Click "Download SVG" buttons below
  2. Go to cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
  3. Convert to PNG at exact sizes (192x192 and 512x512)
  4. Save in public folder

Already have a logo? Just rename your PNG files to icon-192.png and icon-512.png and put them in the public folder!
